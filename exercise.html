Arepa's recipe

  1. Put the Harina pan ( corn flour ) in a bowl
  2. Put cheese in it
  3. Put a bit of butter
  4. Put water
  5. Mix everything
  6. make small arapes ( dish . flat bread )
  7. Put on a hot pan and brown on each side
  8. Enjoy

How to make Margaritas

  1. Put ice in the shaker
  2. Add 50 ml ( white ) Tequila
  3. Add 25 ml Lime juice
  4. Add 25 ml Triple Sec
  5. Shake the shaker
  6. Serve
